## How it's made

Every Wednesday's transcript flows through a pipeline: harvest, intake, classify, synthesize, grade, draft, publish. A heavy track turns buildable ideas into runnable experiments. Ideas are graded 0 to 100; the top 30% form the queue. Each chapter is assembled from the highest-signal ideas in a theme, grounded quarter by quarter in the meetings it came from. Per-theme synthesis lives in themes/THEME-NN/synthesis.md. Every claim traces back to a meeting in transcripts/.

Articles are written in David Olsson's voice. The voice profile is at project-state/voice-profile.md.
