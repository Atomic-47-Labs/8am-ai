## How this is made

Every Wednesday's transcript flows through a skills pipeline — harvest → intake → classify → synthesize → grade → draft → publish. Ideas are graded 0–100; the top 30% form the working queue. Each published chapter is assembled from the highest-signal ideas in a theme, grounded quarter-by-quarter in the meetings it came from. Every claim traces back to a specific meeting in `transcripts/`.
