## How this is made

Every Wednesday's transcript flows through a skills pipeline — harvest → intake → classify → synthesize → grade → draft → publish, with a heavy track that turns buildable ideas into runnable experiments. Ideas are graded 0–100; the top 30% form the working queue. Each chapter is assembled from the highest-signal ideas in a theme, grounded quarter-by-quarter in the meetings it came from. Per-theme synthesis lives in `themes/THEME-NN/synthesis.md`.
